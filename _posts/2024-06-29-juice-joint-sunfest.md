title: Juice Joint plays Sunfest twice and Poachers once in the span of a week
---
Juice Joint is London's jazz fusion band, who has been hosting the open jams at the Poacher's Arms since February 7th of this year.  This is also the first year that Sunfest's global music festival has featured local performances– as Juice Joint christens the brand new UNESCO City of Music Stage.  Members of Juice Joint include Oscar Abley (tenor sax), Miguel Thompson (drums), Nathan Nykor (keys and composer), Caleb Brown (bass), Ben Heffernan (guitar), Russell Freier (guitar), Camilo Borrero (percussion), Ricky Chee (sax), Alexa Brown (trumpet), Charlie Kramers (bass), Evan Chambers (drums), and Andrew Racknor (tenor sax).
